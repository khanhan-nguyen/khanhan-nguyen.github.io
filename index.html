Hi everyone, I'm Anna! 👋

I was born and raised in Vietnam. I am a Computer Science student at Fordham University with interests in software development, data, artificial intelligence, and technology.

Originally, my academic and career path was headed straight toward Finance. I have always loved the strategic side of business, from managing cross-functional teams to overseeing logistics for community-driven projects back in Vietnam.

However, my trajectory completely changed when I participated in a national startup challenge. While driving the product design for a social impact app and working to scale our features down to a clean MVP, I had a massive realization: I didn't just want to manage the business side of products. I wanted to actually build them from the ground up.

A year ago, I printed out my very first "Hello, World." This officially pivoted my decision to change my major to Computer Science. I'm so glad I made the decision. Today, I’m diving deep into algorithms, data analysis, and building foundational projects in C and Python.

I am actively looking for Software Engineering and Product Management internships where I can combine my background in business strategy with my passion for building clean, user-focused software.
